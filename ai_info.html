Artificial Intelligence

AI allows machines to learn, think and make decisions.

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of Computer Science.
  • It enables machines to learn and make decisions.
  • Machine Learning allows systems to learn from data.
  • Deep Learning uses neural networks.
  • Used in assistants, chatbots, and recommendations.
  • Examples include Siri, Google Assistant, and ChatGPT.
  • Applied in healthcare, finance, and transport.
  • Needs large data and computing power.
  • Raises ethical concerns like bias and privacy.
  • Includes Narrow AI and General AI.
